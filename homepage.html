We are Kessoku Band!

"Hey there, we're Kessoku Band! We're a group of four high school girls who are just starting out in the world of music. We may not be the most experienced or polished, but we make up for it with our passion and enthusiasm. We love to play music that's catchy, fun, and full of energy. We're still trying to find our sound, but we're always experimenting and trying new things. We believe that anyone can make music, no matter what their skill level is. So if you're ever feeling shy or insecure about your musical abilities, just remember that we were once in the same boat. So come join us on our musical journey and let's make some noise together!"
